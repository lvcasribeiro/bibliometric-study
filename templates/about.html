About the Usage of the Tools

Merging databases and conducting statistical analysis are essential tools in modern data management. By combining disparate datasets, researchers and analysts can uncover valuable insights, spot trends, and make informed decisions. Statistical analysis aids in extracting meaningful patterns and drawing conclusions, supporting data-driven strategies across various fields.


  1. Steps for Databases Merge Tool:

  2. Using your search strings, perform a search in the Scopus database and export the results in .csv format;
  3. Using your search strings, perform a search in the Web of Science database and export the results in .txt (plain text) format;
  4. Ensure that you export with all fields selected, capturing all possible columns;
  5. Upload both files to the Databases Merge tool and;
  6. Download the results.


  1. Steps for Statistical Analysis Tool:

  2. Upload the resulting spreadsheet obtained from Databases Merge into the Statistical Analysis tool.
